Delicacies

Vigan Longganisa
Garlicky, slightly sweet pork sausage, usually fried and served with garlic rice and egg.
Bagnet
Deep-fried crispy pork belly, crunchy on the outside and tender inside, often served with vinegar dipping sauce.
Empanada
Savory stuffed pastry with grated green papaya, egg, and Vigan longganisa, deep-fried to golden perfection.
Okoy
Shrimp fritters with bean sprouts and vegetables, crispy and served with vinegar dipping sauce.
